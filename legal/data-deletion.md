## Your Right to Data Deletion

Under various privacy regulations (including GDPR and CCPA), you have the right to request the deletion of your personal data. We respect your privacy and make this process straightforward.

---

## What Data We Store

When you use Caymus Tanks Calculator, we may store:

| Data Type | Storage Location | Deletable |
|-----------|------------------|-----------|
| Phone Number | Our servers | ✅ Yes |
| Calculation History | Device + Cloud | ✅ Yes |
| App Preferences | Device + Cloud | ✅ Yes |
| Anonymous Analytics | Our servers | ✅ Yes (upon request) |

---

## How to Delete Your Data

### Option 1: Delete from Within the App

1. Open Caymus Tanks Calculator
2. Go to **Settings** (gear icon)
3. Tap **Account**
4. Tap **Delete My Account**
5. Confirm the deletion

This will immediately:
- Delete your account and phone number from our servers
- Remove all cloud-synced calculation history
- Clear your local app data

### Option 2: Email Request

If you cannot access the app, send an email to:

**privacy@chyrris.com**

Subject: **Data Deletion Request - Caymus Tanks**

Include in your email:
- The phone number associated with your account
- Confirmation that you want all data deleted

We will process your request within **30 days** and send confirmation when complete.

### Option 3: Delete Local Data Only

To clear only local data without deleting your account:

**iOS:**
1. Go to Settings → General → iPhone Storage
2. Find Caymus Tanks
3. Tap "Offload App" or "Delete App"

**Android:**
1. Go to Settings → Apps → Caymus Tanks
2. Tap "Storage"
3. Tap "Clear Data"

---

## What Happens After Deletion

After your data is deleted:

- ✅ Your phone number is removed from our authentication system
- ✅ All calculation history is permanently deleted
- ✅ Your preferences and settings are erased
- ✅ Any subscription is cancelled (you may need to also cancel through App Store/Play Store)

**Note:** Some anonymized, aggregated data may be retained for analytics purposes, but this data cannot be linked back to you.
