# Privacy Policy

**Caymus Tanks Calculator**
**Effective Date:** January 17, 2026
**Last Updated:** January 17, 2026

Chyrris Technologies Inc. ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Caymus Tanks Calculator mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide
- **Account Information:** When you create an account, we collect your phone number for authentication purposes.
- **Usage Data:** Tank calculations, measurement history, and preferences you save within the App.

### 1.2 Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers.
- **Log Data:** App usage patterns, error logs, and performance data.
- **Analytics:** Anonymous usage statistics to improve the App.

### 1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect
- We do not collect precise location data.
- We do not access your contacts, photos, or other personal files.
- We do not collect financial or payment information directly (handled by third-party payment processors).

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Provide and maintain the App's functionality
- Authenticate your identity via phone verification (OTP)
- Save your calculation history and preferences
- Improve and optimize the App's performance
- Communicate with you about updates or support
- Process subscription payments through third-party services

---

## 3. Data Storage and Security

### 3.1 Storage
- Your data is stored securely on cloud servers.
- Calculation history is stored locally on your device and optionally synced to the cloud.

### 3.2 Security Measures
- All data transmissions are encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS protocols.
- We implement access controls and security measures to protect your data.
- Phone numbers are used solely for authentication and are not shared with third parties.

## 5. Data Retention

- **Account Data:** Retained while your account is active. Deleted within 30 days of account deletion request.
- **Calculation History:** Stored locally until you clear it. Cloud-synced data follows account retention.
- **Analytics Data:** Anonymized and retained for up to 24 months.

## 6. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access:** Request a copy of your personal data.
- **Correction:** Update or correct inaccurate information.
- **Deletion:** Request deletion of your account and associated data.
- **Opt-out:** Disable optional analytics and data collection.

To exercise these rights, contact us at: **privacy@chyrris.com**
